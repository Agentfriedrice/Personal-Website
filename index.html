Full Redesign of UCSD's Timekeeping Website: Ecotime

A solo project where I did a full redesign of the Ecotime timekeeping website. Through user research and feedback, I identified that users frequently struggled with finding features due to confusing menus and a cluttered interface. The redesigned website organizes all navigation into a left-aligned sidebar, giving users a clear, always-visible menu with direct access to important functions like logging hours, viewing timesheets, and submitting timesheets. The design also incorporates user-friendly icons and clear labels to enhance clarity and reduce cognitive load.
